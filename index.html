About Me

It always seems difficult to describe what type of person I am. Growing up I seemed to always be somewhat of a class clown. Entertaining others is where I truly strived. While I always had a natural desire to make others laugh, I fell in love with sports at a young age. When I was just 4 years old I began playing soccer, and later began participating in baseball, track & field, wrestling, hockey, football, and MMA. The combination of these sports made me tough and also taught me to be disciplined. Another strong passion of mine is music. In my opinion, music is one of the best things this life has to offer. When I was 18 I picked up a guitar and was obsessed with learning. Music taught me patience and helped me to better understand emotions, as well as to truly feel and process them.

You may still be asking yourself: "But, who is this whiskey-chugging, cigar-smoking, mysterious man named Jake Powers?!" Well, I am a man that has learned a lot about life through countless mistakes and painful heartaches. I have grown a lot through loss and try not to take anything for granted. I cherish my time and the people in my life the most. I am an old soul, nonjudgemental, observant, and a very understanding person. I have a strong drive for the passions in my life. I do not like to let myself, or others, down. I am a very dependable person to those who give me chances and believe in me. I am still learning and growing; constantly trying to better myself every day. But most of all, I strive to inspire, motivate, and help others enjoy life.
